Submitting Work

flexible submission methods for students and teachers

Via Submission Page

Log in to your graded.pro account, go to the "Submit Work" section, choose your subject (e.g., General Science), upload your file, and hit Submit for Grading.

Via Google Classroom

Join your teacher’s class on Google Classroom, find the assigned work, and turn it in as usual — our system will automatically fetch it for AI grading.

Via Mobile App

Open the graded.pro app on your phone, tap “Submit Assignment,” select your subject, upload your file or photo, and submit it directly from your device.
